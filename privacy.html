Privacy Policy

Thoughtfully keeps personal details focused and intentional.

Thoughtfully helps users remember birthdays and save the small details that make showing up for people easier.

Information you save

Thoughtfully lets users save names, birthdays, relationships, reminder choices, favorite details, gift clues, personal notes, profile photos, and meaningful moments.

This information is stored locally on the user's device unless otherwise stated. Thoughtfully does not sell personal information.

Permissions

Thoughtfully only requests permissions when they are needed for a feature the user chooses to use.

Notifications

Notification permission is optional and is used for birthday reminders, optional birthday prep reminders, moment reminders, and gentle app reminders.

Photos

Photo access is optional and is used only when a user chooses to add a profile photo for someone saved in the app.

Contacts

Contact access is optional and is used only when a user chooses to import one selected contact. Thoughtfully only imports available name, birthday, contact photo, and notes for the selected contact.

Thoughtfully does not import phone numbers, email addresses, addresses, company names, or job titles.

Thoughtful Ideas (Experimental)

Thoughtfully can use saved profile details to generate helpful suggestions for birthdays, gifts, and small ways to show up. This feature is experimental and can be turned off anytime in Settings.

When Thoughtful Ideas is used, only selected profile details needed to create suggestions are sent to the backend. Thoughtfully does not send contact photos, phone numbers, email addresses, addresses, company names, job titles, or full contact records for this feature.

Affiliate links

Some shopping links may be affiliate links, which means Thoughtfully may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

Data sharing

Thoughtfully does not sell user data. Thoughtfully may use trusted service providers only when needed to operate app features, such as generating Thoughtful Ideas.
